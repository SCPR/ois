Between Jan. 1, 2010 and Dec. 31, 2014, the Justice System Integrity Division of the Los Angeles County District Attorney's Office responded to 458 shootings involving on-duty and off-duty police officers and sheriff's deputies.

Armed vs. Unarmed

For about 81 percent of those shootings over the five-year period, KPCC has reviewed DA letters sent to law enforcement agencies that lay out why the agency has declined to prosecute officers.
